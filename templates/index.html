About the Open Journal Club

The open journal club is an online literature discussion forum. Its aim is to develop literature mining and discussion skills.

The Open Journal Club is a webapp powered by Google App Engine and Crocodoc. To participate you need to login through a Google or Yahoo account.

The Open Journal Club consists of discussion threads, each attempting to answer a single question. As a participant, you can leave comments and annotate uploaded scientific papers. You can also upload pdf documents and propose your own topic of discussion, but for this you have to .

A good place to start is the didactic thread How to use the Open Journal Club?. It is meant as a practice and testing place, so feel free to leave commentsi (however relevant) and practice annotating pdf documents in place.

The Open Journal Club is written in Python and is open source. Please don't hesitate to fork, copy or steal the code from Github.
